Our Commitment to Sustainability

Local Sourcing

We partner with local farmers to source the freshest seasonal ingredients. By supporting local agriculture, we reduce transportation emissions and strengthen our community's economy.

  • ✓ Partnerships with 10+ local farms
  • ✓ Seasonal menu rotation

Zero Waste

Our kitchen practices minimize food waste through careful planning, composting, and creative use of ingredients. We're proud to be working towards a zero-waste future.

  • ✓ Commercial composting program
  • ✓ Food waste tracking system
  • ✓ Weekly waste audits

Eco-Friendly Packaging

All our takeout containers are biodegradable and made from recycled materials. We're committed to reducing single-use plastics and promoting sustainable packaging solutions.

  • ✓ 100% compostable containers
  • ✓ Plant-based utensils
  • ✓ Recycled paper products
